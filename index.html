Thinking about diving into cryptocurrency? Check out these 3 top options to get started!

BITCOIN

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that enables peer-to-peer transactions without relying on intermediaries like banks. Launched in 2009 by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto, it operates on a secure and transparent blockchain. With a fixed supply of 21 million coins, Bitcoin is widely considered a store of value and remains the most recognized and influential cryptocurrency in the world of digital finance.

ETHEREUM

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain platform that allows developers to build and deploy smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps). Launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin, Ethereum uses its native cryptocurrency, Ether (ETH), for transactions and network fees. It introduced programmable blockchain technology, making it a critical foundation for decentralized finance (DeFi), NFTs, and blockchain-based innovations.

LITECOIN

Litecoin (LTC)

Litecoin is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency created in 2011 by Charlie Lee as a faster and more scalable alternative to Bitcoin. Using a similar blockchain structure, Litecoin offers faster transaction speeds and lower fees. Known as "the silver to Bitcoin’s gold," Litecoin is designed for efficient, everyday use, and continues to play a significant role in the cryptocurrency ecosystem with widespread adoption.
